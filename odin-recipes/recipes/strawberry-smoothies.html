Strawberry Smoothies

This strawberry smoothie recipe blends strawberries with milk, yogurt, sugar, and vanilla for an icy-cold treat. You'll love it!



Ingredients

  • 8 strawberries, hulled
  • ½ cup skim milk
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 6 cubes ice, crushed

Directions

Step 1
Gather all ingredients.

Step 2
Combine strawberries, milk, yogurt, sugar, and vanilla in a blender. Add ice and blend until smooth and creamy.

Step 3
Pour into glasses and serve immediately. Enjoy!
